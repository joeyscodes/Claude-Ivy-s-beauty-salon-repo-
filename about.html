How It Began

A VISION FOR
EVERY WOMAN

Ivy's Beauty Secret was founded with one belief — that every woman deserves to feel extraordinary, regardless of the occasion. What began as a small studio in Musaffah has grown into Abu Dhabi's most trusted destination for hair, makeup, bridal beauty, skin care, and relaxation.

Every detail of the salon — from the products we choose to the beauticians we train — is selected with one question in mind: will this make a woman feel like the best version of herself?

"We don't just style hair or apply makeup. We help women walk out feeling like the queens they already are."
