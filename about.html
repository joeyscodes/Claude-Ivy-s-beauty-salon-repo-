How We Began

Built for the woman who
never gets to be first

Ivy's Beauty Secret opened in Musaffah with one purpose — to give every woman in our community a place that puts her first. Not a quick appointment squeezed between errands, but a studio where she is genuinely cared for.

From hair colour correction to bridal preparation, our specialists trained across hair, beauty, skin and nails so that one visit can cover every detail of how you want to look and feel.

Today, "Be a Pampered Princess" isn't just our tagline — it's the standard we hold every appointment to.
